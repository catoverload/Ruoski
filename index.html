!ALERT! Three cases of meningococcal infection (meningitis) have been reported in the local county of Dorset.
A temporary medical clinic has been opened at Budmouth Academy. These incidents are not related to the Kent problem.
Authorities are urging awareness of typical meningitis symptoms:
- Fever
- Headache
- Vomiting
- Sensitivity to light
- Non-blanching rash
